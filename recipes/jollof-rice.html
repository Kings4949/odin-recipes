Jollof-Rice

Make a sumptuous rice mixed with stew and other ingredients for about forty-five minutes and enjoy the meal by serving while hot. The meal is in high demand at parties.It is one of my favourite. It is a very popular food which everyone would love. I will recommend you prepare and eat every week

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sliced Onions
  • Pepper
  • Maggi
  • Fresh meat and fish
  • Groundnut oil

Cooking instructions

Step 1

Wash the rice with clean water and Perboiled the rice for about 5 minutes and drain out the boiling water with a fitter.

Step 2

Put the perboiled rice in a fitter and cover it to prevent flies

Step 3

Boil the meat and fish in the pot and add tomato paste to the mixture. Stir it to dissolve

Step 4

Add salt, sliced onions, maggi, pepper and cook for about twenty minutes

Step 5

Remove the fish and meat from the mixture and put a frying pan on the fire

Step 6

put some quantity of groundnut oil in the frying pan and heat briefly.

Step 7

Add the fish and meat carefully. After sometime when it is fully fried, remove the frying pan from the fire and remove the fish and meat

Step 8

Put the pot containing the tomatoe mixture on the fire then pour the perboiled rice from the filter into the mixture and add water. Then continue to cook for sometime and finally add the fried meat and fish and remove from fire when the it is getting dried and serve while hot
