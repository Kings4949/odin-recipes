Beans Porridge

Enjoy the pest non animal protein from plant and stay healthy with this delcious meal which will make you feel dizzy and lazy after consumption. It will boost your immune system with all the rich content from protein. Commonly consume domestically. Preparation time can take about one hour

Ingredients

  • Red Beans
  • Yam
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Palm oil
  • Maggi

Cooking Instructions

Step 1

Boil the beans for about 20 minutes and peel and add slices of yam

Step 2

Add crayfish, wash and slice the tomatoes and as well as onions and add to the mixture in the pot, pepper and continue to cook

Step 3

Then add blended pepper and a cube of maggi.

Step 4

After sometime when fully cooked add small salt and palm oil taste and remove from the flame

Step 5

Serve while hot and enjoy!
